Quick Cook Oatmeal

Ingredients:

  • Base Recipe
    • ¬Ω cup rolled oats
    • 1 cup water
    • Pinch salt
    • 2-3 tablespoons milk of choice
  • Strawberry
    • ¬Ω cup strawberries sliced
    • 1 tablespoon half and half
    • 2 teaspoons honey
    • 1/8 teaspoon vanilla extract

Cooking Instructions:

  • Microwave
    1. Place the oats, water, and salt in a microwave-safe bowl.
    2. Heat in the microwave on high for 90 seconds.
    3. If needed, add 15-second increments until soft.
    4. Add milk and stir before serving.
  • Stove Top
    1. Bring water to a boil in a small saucepan.
    2. Add oats, reduce heat, and cook for 5 minutes.
    3. Add milk, cover, and let sit for 2-3 minutes.

Assembly:

  1. Stir in the toppings and let rest to cool slightly.
  2. If needed, add more milk for texture.
  3. Serve warm.

Health Benefits of Oatmeal

Oatmeal is high in fiber, which helps with digestion and keeps you full longer. It supports heart health by lowering cholesterol and provides lasting energy throughout the day.

Tasty Topping Ideas

Top your oatmeal with fresh fruits like bananas or berries. Nuts and seeds give crunch, while honey, maple syrup, or cinnamon add flavor and sweetness.
