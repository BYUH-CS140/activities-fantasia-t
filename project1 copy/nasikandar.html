Nasi Kandar Pelita

Nasi Kandar Pelita is one of Malaysia’s most popular Indian-Muslim (Mamak) restaurant chains, best known for its rich and flavorful nasi kandar. Originating from Penang, nasi kandar is a traditional Malaysian dish that features steamed rice served with a variety of curries, meats, vegetables, and gravies. Pelita is praised for its generous portions, affordable prices, and its ability to maintain authentic taste across all its outlets.

The restaurant is always bustling with locals and tourists looking for a true taste of Malaysian comfort food. Customers can choose from dishes like ayam goreng (fried chicken), sotong (squid), beef curry, and telur rebus (boiled eggs), all drenched in a delicious mix of thick, spicy sauces.
