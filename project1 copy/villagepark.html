Village Park Restaurant

Village Park Restaurant is a household name in Malaysia, particularly famous for its award-winning nasi lemak. Located in Damansara Uptown, Selangor, this restaurant has gained a loyal following for serving what many call the best nasi lemak in the country. The highlight is their crispy fried chicken served alongside fragrant coconut rice, sambal, anchovies, peanuts, and a boiled egg.

The restaurant is often packed during breakfast and lunch hours, with customers coming from all around to get a taste of this Malaysian classic. Its consistency, fast service, and authentic flavors have made Village Park a must-visit eatery for food lovers visiting Kuala Lumpur.
