Jalan Alor Night Market

Jalan Alor is one of Kuala Lumpur’s most famous food streets, offering a wide array of street food and traditional Malaysian dishes. Located in the Bukit Bintang area, the night market comes alive after sundown with dozens of food stalls serving everything from satay to noodles, grilled meats, and fresh seafood.

The street is always buzzing with locals and tourists alike, all looking to enjoy authentic Malaysian street food in a vibrant and lively atmosphere. The market is also known for its late-night food options, making it a perfect place for a midnight snack or meal. It’s a must-visit destination for food lovers in Kuala Lumpur.

Address:
Jalan Alor, Bukit Bintang, 50200 Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

Opening Hours:
Daily: 5:00 PM – 12:00 AM

Admission Rates:
Free entry (cost of food varies by stall)
