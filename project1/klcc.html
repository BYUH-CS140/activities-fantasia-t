KLCC Park

KLCC Park is a beautifully landscaped park located in the heart of Kuala Lumpur, right next to the iconic Petronas Twin Towers. It features lush green spaces, scenic walking paths, a large lake, and the famous Symphony Lake, where visitors can enjoy a spectacular water fountain show. The park is a popular spot for both locals and tourists, offering a peaceful retreat amidst the hustle and bustle of the city.

The park also includes a playground, a wading pool for children, and a jogging track, making it a great location for outdoor activities. Visitors can relax and unwind by the lake, take photos of the Petronas Twin Towers from the park’s many viewpoints, or enjoy the tranquil ambiance of the surroundings.

Address:
KLCC Park, Kuala Lumpur City Centre, 50088 Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

Opening Hours:
Daily: 7:00 AM – 10:00 PM

Admission Rates:
Free entry
