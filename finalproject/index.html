Why 10MinuteTastes?

As students ourselves at BYU–Hawaii, we understand how hard it is to balance classes, work, and life. Too often, we skip meals or eat unhealthy snacks because we “don’t have time.”

That’s why this site exists—to help people like us eat well without wasting time or money.
