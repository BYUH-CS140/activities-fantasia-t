Quick Cook Oatmeal

Ingredients:

  • Base Recipe
    • ½ cup rolled oats
    • 1 cup water
    • Pinch salt
    • 2-3 tablespoons milk of choice
  • Strawberry
    • ½ cup strawberries sliced
    • 1 tablespoon half and half
    • 2 teaspoons honey
    • 1/8 teaspoon vanilla extract

Cooking Instructions:

  • Microwave
    1. Place the oats, water, and salt in a microwave-safe bowl.
    2. Heat in the microwave on high for 90 seconds.
    3. If needed, add 15-second increments until the oatmeal is puffed and softened.
    4. This step is only necessary the first time you make it, after which you can gauge the exact time needed.
    5. Add milk and stir before serving.
  • Stove Top
    1. In a small saucepan, bring the water to a boil.
    2. Reduce the heat to low and pour in the oats.
    3. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the oats are soft and have absorbed most of the liquid, about 5 minutes.
    4. Add the milk, remove from heat, cover, and let stand for 2-3 minutes.

Assembly:

  1. Stir in the toppings and let rest for a few minutes to cool.
  2. If desired, thin with a little more milk.
  3. Serve warm.
